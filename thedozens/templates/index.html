Overview

The Dozen's API intends to serve as a humorous, joyful piece of African-American culture where, as a community, good-hearted jokes are shared and enjoyed. To this end, please submit a joke for review if you feel its presence is a disservice to that end.

Review Process

Depending on the reason for the review process, a contributor to the API will review the submitted joke within seven working days.


In the event of any submission reason other than 'Inappropriate,' a single contributor will review the joke's content and comments provided in this submission and take appropriate action. Appropriate actions include but are not2 limited to the following:

  1. Joke Reclassification - Changing the Joke from explicit or inexplicit
  2. Joke Recatagorizion - Changing the Catagory of the Joke to a more accurate category
  3. Joke Removal - Complete removal from the Joke Database
